 \begin{definition}
  A principal $G$-bundle over $B$ is a $K$-scheme $X$ with a morphism $q: X \to B$ and an action $\alpha: G \times X \to X$ such that $q$ is $G$-invariant:

    G \times X \arrow[r, "\alpha"] \arrow[d, "p_2"] & X \arrow[d, "q"] \\
    X \arrow[r, "q"] & B

 \end{definition}
