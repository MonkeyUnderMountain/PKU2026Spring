This is a class given by Professor Suzuki in the spring semester of 2026 at Peking University. 
I copy the notes from the blackboard and write them in \LaTeX.
The notes are not complete, and I will update them when I have time.

I use the following notations in the notes:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \(\kk\) to denote the base field, and \(\kkk\) is its algebraic closure.
    \item let \(X\) be a variety over \(\kk\), and \(x \in X\) is a point, we denote by \(\fkk(X)\) the function field of \(X\), and \(\rkk(x)\) the residue field of \(x\).
\end{itemize}
